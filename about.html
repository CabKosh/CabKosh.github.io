About Moolah-lah Fundraising

Our Purpose

Moolah-lah Fundraising was created to help elementary and Title 1 schools successfully plan and run fundraising events without stress or confusion. Many schools lack the time, staff, and resources to organize effective fundraisers, and our service is designed to solve that problem.

Why Schools Should Use Us

Schools benefit from our structured, easy-to-follow fundraising system. We provide event planning, marketing support, scheduling, and prize coordination so staff can focus on students while we handle the logistics. Our goal is to maximize fundraising results while reducing workload.

What Makes Us Different

Unlike traditional fundraising programs, we focus on accessibility for underfunded schools and simplify the entire process from start to finish. Every event is designed to be engaging, fun, and profitable.
