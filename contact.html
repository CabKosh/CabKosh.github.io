About Moolah-lah Fundraising

Our Purpose

Moolah-lah Fundraising helps schools, especially Title 1 schools, plan and run successful fundraising events with less stress. We understand that many schools lack the time and resources to organize effective fundraisers, so we provide a complete solution.

Why Schools Choose Us

Our system simplifies fundraising by providing planning, marketing, scheduling, and support. Schools can focus on students while we handle the logistics, resulting in better outcomes and higher participation.

Our Mission

Our goal is to make fundraising accessible, effective, and enjoyable for every school, regardless of budget or experience level.
